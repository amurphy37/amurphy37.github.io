About Me

Solutions-driven full-stack developer applying SaaS background and strong technical skillset towards designing, building and testing functional web applications. Over the past four years in working directly with customers to uncover current challenges in recruiting and marketing, I’ve cultivated a passion for harnessing the power of data and creativity to solve key pain points previously preventing business leaders from reaching their goals. Seeking to further my passion in problem-solving, I returned to the classroom to complete training through the Case Western Reserve University Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp. After bolstering my front-end, database management, and server-side capabilities in mastering React.js, MySQL, Mongo, Node.js and Express, I worked to design, develop and implement TrailTails, a dog-focused hiking community, and DreamJob, an organization tool to aid job seekers in finding and tracking job applications and am now seeking to continue to combine my sales experience with my technical knowledge in a team-oriented software development role.

While not working on odd projects I continue to follow my passion in music and cooking, making mixes and honing my skills in the kitchen.
